What is the dilution process?
Dilution of the solution is the process of decreading the concentration of a solute in a solution by mixing it with more solvent like adding more water to the solution. In simple words to dolute a solution means to add more solvent without adding more solute.
How to calculate dilution process?
To calculate dilution process you should use the following formula:
M1 * V1 = M2 * V2
where:
M1 = initial molarity
V1 = initial volume in literes
M2 = final molarity
V2 = final volume in liters
Why use dilution process?
The dilution process is used in order to reduce the concentration of the solute.
